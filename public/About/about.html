About Me

To give you an idea of who I am as a person, I'd like to highlight a few things on this page. First of all, I'm quite a social person and I regularly am looking to meet friends in and around work. I have always relished the importance of making new friends which helps when working in a larger team or company. I would say I am a very easy person to get to know, and also to liaise with.

I am consistently honest with myself, as well as others too, which helps me to improve in different areas. Something I very much live by is to treat people how you want to be treated, and you can see this in how I carry myself day to day. I certainly wish to treat people fairly and create positive, beneficial relationships where-ever possible.

My main passions include playing video games online with friends, being out in nature and playing sports. I was raised on many a camping holiday and always enjoy going for walks, runs and just generally exploring new places in the UK. I've been lucky enough to do a summer camp in Quebec, Canada as a councilor. This was post university which embodied a lot of the things I enjoy, like being out and being proactive. The main sports I follow are football, volleyball and badminton currently. However, I mainly only play badminton regularly - the social side of it all is a big part of it for me, as well as keeping healthy and looking after myself.

Why am I a good person to work with?

  • My favorite work environments are ones where there is always something to do, I like being proactive and contributing as much as possible. This is not only to benefit my employer, but also my fellow employees.
  • Previous employers would credit my work ethic and also, my ability to slot in where needed learning along the way.
  • My approachability enables me to get to know colleagues quickly, to help build trust within our working relationship.
  • Something that I really value above many other things is remaining as easy to work with as possible, by being well spoken, polite and open minded.
