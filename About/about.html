About Me

To give you an idea of who I am as a person, I'd like to highlight a few things on this page. First of all, I'm quite a social person and I regularly am looking to meet friends in and around work. By being honest with myself, I am a very self aware as a person. I always strive to be as good to others as I can be, whether that be within a professional setting or how I handle myself when I am out and about. Something I very much live by is to treat people how you want to be treated, and you can see this in how I carry myself day to day.

My main passions include playing video games online with friends, being out in nature and playing sports. The main sports I follow are football, volleyball and badminton currently, but I mainly only play badminton regularly - the social side of it all is a big part of it for me, as well as keeping healthy and looking after myself.
